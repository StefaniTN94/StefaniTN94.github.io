About In a Search of Intelligent life

In a Search of Intelligent Life is inspired on "Search for extraterrestrial intelligence" Institute. It's time to also search for the future Intelligence, here on Earth. This blog is about all the exciting news that prove how great human race can be, and about tracking the evolution of out technologies. The creator of this blog is Stefani Naydenova, currently working as Hardware Support for IBM, and also she is studying programming at Software University.
